Cities Weather

In this website, you'll be seeing a visualization of the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator. To accomplish this, it is utilized a simple Python library, the OpenWeatherMap API, and a little common sense to create a representative model of weather across world cities. The objective is to build a series of scatter plots to showcase the following relationships between 500 random cities:

  • Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
